    Me, my art and you.

    My art to me, is a way to put myself out there, to the world. I want to be able to have some contact with humanity, I guess, because I live in a life that isn't very much an open book. That's why I love being able to work with words and thoughts, having people read, listen or look at my work, interpreting it their own ways. Doing what I do, I give myself a chance to express myself and bring meaning to something that may seem meaningless to most. It 's nice knowing that I can make someone smile, feel happy or just enjoy it. I would not consider myself someone special, if anything it's you who's special for letting me do what I do. So thanks, in a way. My art will always be there, waiting for the chance that someone will come across it and have a look, maybe enjoy it, maybe even laugh! This is my way to communicate with you, without physically being together, but to have that direct communication between us. So to say, art is my voice.
